Kindred Nutrition - Women's Wellness and Nutrition

Kindred Nutrition is a new virtual women’s wellness and nutrition business. They needed help with customizing their WordPress template site to their brand and also integrating their site with WooCommerce to sell medical supplements. I edited the WordPress template with custom CSS to make the look and feel better aligned, set up a newsletter to convert clients, integrated WooCommerce into the site and made the site mobile responsive. The end product was a beautiful and easily navigable site where users could book appointments as well as buy products.

Hourly Kapital - Early-stage Startup

Hourly Kapital is an early-stage SaaS (software as a service) startup working to increase company productivity by outsourcing tasks to internal employees. I engineered a marketplace SaaS Minimum Viable Product for the founder. I was given sketches and specifications of the app and I designed the data models and user flow of the website. I also suggested and implemented important features that helped the user access information in one central place. I deployed it to Heroku and it was beta tested by several interested companies.

Shopify

New Harvest Coffee Roasters - Wholesale Coffee

I helped New Harvest improve their online customer experience as they moved their business model to rely more on online sales. They were losing customers because the process of logging into customer accounts and changing their coffee subscriptions was too difficult. I made the process of logging into accounts to change subscription preferences seamless, fixed an issue with customers receiving shipping charges over a certain limit and redesigned their blog. After improving their site they were prepared for the demand of the holiday season and had one less thing to worry about.
